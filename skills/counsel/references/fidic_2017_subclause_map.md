# FIDIC 2017 Suite — Sub-Clause Map for Refining/Petrochem EPC

## The Three Rainbow Books (2017 Edition)
| Book | Color | Use Case | Risk Allocation | Pakistan Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Red Book** (Construction) | Red | Employer-designed, contractor-built (BoQ measurement) | Employer takes design risk | OGRA pipelines, downstream incremental works |
| **Yellow Book** (Plant & Design-Build) | Yellow | Contractor designs to Employer's Requirements | Contractor takes design + execution risk | Refinery upgrades, process plant additions |
| **Silver Book** (EPC/Turnkey) | Silver | Single-point lump sum turnkey | Contractor takes virtually all risk | Greenfield refinery/petrochem, hydrogen, off-site/utilities packages |

### Dispute Avoidance / Adjudication Board (DAAB) — NEW in 2017
- **Mandatory standing board** of 1 or 3 members (vs ad-hoc DAB in 1999)
- Constituted within 28 days of contract effectiveness
- Members visit site quarterly for "Dispute Avoidance" meetings
- Decisions binding pending final arbitration; "Notice of Dissatisfaction" required within 28 days to preserve arbitration right

## Silver Book vs Yellow Book — Risk Decision Matrix
**Silver Book preferred when:**
- Lender financeability demands turnkey delivery
- Owner has limited technical staff for ongoing oversight
- Site conditions are well-characterized
- Process licensor terms permit single-package delivery

**Yellow Book preferred when:**
- Process license terms require Employer mediation
- Site conditions uncertain (subsurface, brownfield interfaces)
- Multiple contracts will interface (utilities, offsites, process)
- Employer wants design review milestones

**Critical Silver Book risk premium**: 8-15% on contract price for risk transfer; **don't accept Silver if total value <USD 200-300M** — diseconomy of scale.

## Pakistan-Specific Modifications (Common)
- Sub-Clause 1.13 (Compliance with Laws) — expanded list: NEQS, OGRA SOPs, IEPS, Sindh-EPA NOCs, SPS rules
- Sub-Clause 6.4 (Labor Laws) — Sindh/Punjab Industrial Relations Acts, Workers' Children Welfare Cess, ECC pensions
- Sub-Clause 13.7 (Tax adjustments) — Pakistan Federal/Provincial sales tax, customs duty changes, deemed duty allocation under BRP-21
- Sub-Clause 14.15 (Currency) — Critical to fix invoice currency and CBR/repatriation provisions (SBP foreign exchange manual)
