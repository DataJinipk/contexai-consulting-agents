# Pakistan REIT Regulations 2022 — Structuring Brief

## Authority
- **SECP** — Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan
- Anchored in REIT Regulations 2022 (replacing 2015 Regulations)
- Amended via SRO 1080(I)/2024 and Finance Act 2025

## REIT Categories
| Category | Purpose | Min. Asset | Listing |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rental REIT** | Income from stabilised real estate | PKR 5 bn fund size, ≥80% in income-producing real estate | Mandatory PSX |
| **Developmental REIT** | Development + sale; can transition to Rental | PKR 5 bn fund size, ≤80% in development assets | Mandatory PSX |
| **Hybrid REIT** | Mix of rental + development | PKR 5 bn fund size, blend | Mandatory PSX |
| **PP REIT** (Private Placement) | Closed-ended, accredited investors | PKR 1 bn min, ≤50 investors | No PSX listing required (since 2024) |

## Mandatory Structural Parties
1. **Sponsor** — promotes the scheme; min 25% retention for 2 years post-listing; net worth ≥PKR 200M; "Fit & Proper" SECP test
2. **REIT Management Company (RMC)** — SECP-licensed under NBFC Regulations; min paid-up capital PKR 200M; min CEO experience 7+ yrs; min sponsor of RMC net worth PKR 500M
3. **Trustee** — independent of Sponsor/RMC; CDC Trustee Services, MCB-FSL, or scheduled bank's trustee arm; holds title in trust for unitholders
4. **Custodian** — for financial assets (often same as Trustee)
5. **Property Valuer** — PBA-recognized + on SECP panel; mandatory revaluation annually
6. **Statutory Auditor** — Big-4 or QCR-rated Cat-A firm
7. **Shariah Advisor** — if Islamic REIT

## Tax Treatment (Pakistan ITO 2001, post-FA 2025)
| Item | Rate / Treatment |
|---|---|
| REIT corporate income | **Exempt** if ≥90% net income distributed to unitholders |
| Distribution by REIT | Subject to **15% withholding tax** at REIT level (final tax for non-corporate unitholders) |
| Capital gains by REIT | **Exempt** if held >12 months and ≥90% distributed |
| Stamp duty on transfer of property to REIT | **Reduced**: 0.5% (vs typical 3-5%) — incentive provision |
| CVT (Capital Value Tax) | **Exempt** on REIT-asset transfers — provincial-by-province variation |
| Super Tax (§4C ITO) | Applies only at unitholder level if corporate holder |

## Listing Requirements (PSX REIT Counter)
- Free float ≥25% post-listing
- Min 200 unitholders at IPO
- Lock-in: 50% of Sponsor units locked for 2 years
- Quarterly NAV publication
- Annual auditor's certification on yield calculation
- 90% net rental income distribution mandatory

## Sponsor Sale-Leaseback Economics (Industrial REIT)
- Sale-leaseback typically priced at 7-9% cap rate (Lahore/Karachi industrial)
- Sponsor receives PKR X cash upfront; leases back at fixed rent (CPI + 3% escalator typical)
- Tax: Sale to REIT triggers CGT on Sponsor side (15% if held <1 yr, 12.5% / 10% / 7.5% / 0% for >1/2/3/4 yr holds) — but stamp duty reduction is sweetener
- IFRS 16: Sponsor recognizes ROU asset + lease liability; cash inflow recorded as sale gain or finance liability depending on transfer-of-control test (IFRS 15)

## Anchor Investor Strategy (Pakistan)
**Tier-1 anchors** (PKR 200M+ tickets): Mutual funds (NIT, HBL Asset Mgmt, MCB Arif Habib, UBL Funds, AKD), Pension funds (NIT, EOBI, provincial pension funds), Family offices (textile barons, real-estate magnates)
**Tier-2 anchors** (PKR 50-200M tickets): Insurance companies (EFU, Jubilee, State Life subsidiaries), Provident funds, Bank treasuries
**Tier-3 anchors** (PKR 5-50M tickets): HNIs via brokerage allocations, REIT-focused funds

**Anchor pricing leverage** — Anchor commitment of 30-40% of IPO size typically enables pricing at top of indicative range; lower anchor coverage forces deeper discount or postpone.

## Closing Timeline (Typical Industrial REIT)
| Phase | Duration | Key Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Pre-launch (RMC mobilisation) | 3-4 months | RMC incorporation, SECP license, Sponsor F&P clearance, Trust Deed drafting |
| 2. SECP REIT registration | 2-3 months | Offering Document, Constitutive Documents, asset transfer agreements |
| 3. Pre-IPO roadshow | 1-2 months | Anchor pre-commitments, valuation finalisation, listing prospectus |
| 4. PSX listing | 1-2 months | Bookbuilding (if applicable), allotment, trading |
| **Total** | **7-11 months** | First yield distribution 90 days post-listing |
