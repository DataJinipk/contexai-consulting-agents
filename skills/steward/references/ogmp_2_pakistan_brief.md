# OGMP 2.0 — Pakistan Implementation Brief

## Framework Overview
The Oil and Gas Methane Partnership 2.0 (OGMP 2.0) is the United Nations Environment Programme's flagship reporting framework for upstream, midstream and downstream methane emissions. It comprises **5 Levels** of progressive granularity:

| Level | Scope | Method | Pakistan Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Corporate aggregate | Generic emission factors | Mari, OGDCL, PPL signed |
| L2 | Source-type aggregate | Country-tier factors | OGDCL pilot scope |
| L3 | Source-type, asset-level | Detailed inventory | Mari Petroleum 2026 target |
| L4 | Source-level, asset-level | Direct measurement | Mari L4 commitment (2026-27) |
| L5 | Site-level reconciliation | Top-down vs bottom-up | None in Pakistan yet |

## OGMP 2.0 Source Categories (Upstream)
1. Pneumatic devices (continuous bleed, intermittent vent, low-bleed)
2. Compressor seals (centrifugal wet/dry seal; reciprocating rod-packing)
3. Tank vents (flash, working, breathing)
4. Glycol dehydrators (reboiler vent, regenerator vent)
5. Pipeline blowdowns (planned + unplanned)
6. Wellhead/casinghead vents (sour gas, separator)
7. Fugitive equipment leaks (LDAR survey scope)
8. Incomplete flaring (combustion efficiency <98%)
9. Engine/turbine slip (unburned methane in exhaust)

## Pakistan Methane Profile (Estimates)
- Upstream O&G methane: ~40-60 Bcf/yr equivalent of vent + leak losses (UNEP IMEO 2024)
- Largest sources: Mari (Daharki), Sui field (legacy), Qadirpur (high CO₂/methane co-production)
- LDAR penetration: <15% of upstream operators (vs 100% Norway, 90% Canada)

## L4 Build-Out Plan (Typical)
**Year 1**: Baseline source inventory · install continuous monitors (TDLAS, OGI cameras) on top-20 emitters · install LDAR program at all compressor stations.
**Year 2**: Deploy aerial/satellite reconciliation (GHGSat, Bluefield) · drone-based OGI annual sweeps · pneumatic device replacement program (high-bleed → low-bleed → electric).
**Year 3**: Continuous-monitor network expansion · L5 site-level reconciliation pilot at flagship field.

## Capex Bands (Pakistan)
- L3 baseline inventory + OGI camera program: USD 0.8-1.2 M per field
- L4 continuous monitoring (TDLAS + OGI + drones): USD 3-5 M per field
- Pneumatic device replacement (200-400 devices/field): USD 1.5-3 M
- LDAR program annual run-rate: USD 250-400k/yr per field

## OGMP 2.0 Reporting Cadence
- Annual reporting to UNEP IMEO by 30 April
- L4 verification through 3rd-party (DNV, Bureau Veritas, ERM)
- Gold Standard award for L4/L5 with year-on-year reduction trajectory
