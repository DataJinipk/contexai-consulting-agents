# Pakistan NEQS — National Environmental Quality Standards

## Authority
- **Pak-EPA** (Pakistan Environmental Protection Agency) federal coordinator
- **Sindh-EPA / Punjab-EPA / KPK-EPA / Balochistan-EPA** provincial enforcement
- Anchored in Pakistan Environmental Protection Act 1997 (as amended)

| COD | 150 | 400 | 400 |

## Industry-Specific Limits Beyond NEQS
- **Petroleum refineries** — IEPS (Industry-specific Effluent and Emission Standards) draft 2018: tighter benzene-air (5 ppm), VOC-effluent (specific BTEX limits), oily-water separator effluent ≤5 mg/L oil/grease.
- **Petrochemicals** — Sindh Industrial Estates Authority + NEQS combo; ZLD (zero liquid discharge) increasingly demanded by Sindh-EPA in new permits.

## Enforcement Triggers
- IEE/EIA required for any project >PKR 50M capex or >5 acres (SEPA-2014 Regulations).
- Self-Monitoring & Reporting (SMART) program — quarterly submissions to provincial EPA.
- Non-compliance penalty: PKR 100k/day + closure orders + criminal liability under §17 EPA-1997.
